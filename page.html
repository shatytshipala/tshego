"The purpose of the Media Studies examines the to examine the complicated interactions which exist between people and the cultures that they consume. It is intended to prompt an individual to critically think about how they engage with, comprehend and are influenced by the media on a daily basis. In order for one to understand this module and the effects that media has on society, he or she must be media literate. This module focuses on the concepts that are already known to the reader, however, it requires the reader to have a more critical approach on how he or she interacts with the media by making reference to what ideologies are and how they shape the way media is represented. In order for one to build a critical and ethical application to this module, it is necessary to learn how to choose and apply theories to daily media engagement.
